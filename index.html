My Favorite Movies

Y Tu Mamá También

In Mexico, two teenage boys and an attractive older woman embark on a road trip.

Annie Hall

Neurotic New York comedian Alvy Singer falls in love with the ditsy Annie Hall.

Inside Llewyn Davis

A week in the life of a young singer as he navigates the Greenwich Village folk scene of 1961.

The Motorcycle Diaries

The dramatization of a motorcycle road trip Che Guevara went on in his youth.

Gravity

A medical engineer and an astronaut work together to survive after a catastrophe destroys their shuttle and leaves them adrift in orbit.

The Social Network

Harvard student Mark Zuckerberg creates the social networking site that would become known as Facebook.

Midnight in Paris

While on a trip to Paris with his fiancée's family, a screenwriter finds himself mysteriously going back to the 1920s every day at midnight.

The One I Love

Struggling with a marriage on the brink of falling apart, a couple escapes for a weekend only to discover an unusual dilemma that awaits them.

Fargo

Jerry Lundegaard's inept crime falls apart due to his and his henchmen's bungling and the persistent police work of the quite pregnant Marge Gunderson.
